# Data Folder

## Public Data (Available Now)
- `sourashtra_phrasebook.csv` - The RAG phrasebook.

## Private Data (Not Uploaded to GitHub)
- `full_audio/` - The 1,800 high-fidelity WAV files for voice cloning.
- `full_dataset.csv` - The complete mapping of audio files to Romanized text.

**Note:** The full audio dataset is stored on a private Google Drive to prevent unauthorized commercial use until our research paper is published.

## Sample Data
- `sample_audio/` - Contains 10 demonstration audio files to showcase quality.
